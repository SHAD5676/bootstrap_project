About DMPI

Dhaka Mohila Polytechnic Institute is a government technical institution dedicated to promoting women's education in engineering and technology. Located in Sher-e-Bangla Nagar, Dhaka, it provides diploma courses in multiple technologies, aiming to empower women for the future workforce.

Campus Life at DMPI

The campus of Dhaka Mohila Polytechnic Institute is a vibrant and inspiring place for students. Surrounded by greenery and a peaceful environment, it offers the perfect setting for learning and creativity. The institute has well-equipped classrooms, modern laboratories, a digital library, and Wi-Fi connectivity across the campus. Students enjoy various extracurricular activities such as cultural programs, sports competitions, and technical festivals that help them grow in both academic and personal areas. The friendly atmosphere and dedicated teachers make the campus a second home for every student.
