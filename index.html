About DMPI

Dhaka Mohila Polytechnic Institute (DMPI) is a prestigious government institution dedicated to empowering women through quality technical and vocational education. Located in Sher-e-Bangla Nagar, Dhaka, it offers diploma programs in various engineering disciplines to prepare skilled, confident, and future-ready professionals.

With modern facilities, experienced teachers, and a supportive campus environment, DMPI ensures a perfect balance of academic excellence and practical learning. The institute continues to inspire women to become leaders in technology, innovation, and development.
