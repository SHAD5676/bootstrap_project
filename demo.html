About Dhaka Mohila Polytechnic Institute

Dhaka Mohila Polytechnic Institute (DMPI) is a government technical institution for women located in Sher-e-Bangla Nagar, Dhaka. It provides diploma engineering courses in various technologies to empower women in technical fields.
